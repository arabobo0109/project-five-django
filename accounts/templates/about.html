My name is Sarah, and I am the creator of Do Life Yourself.

The blog was borne of my DIY attitute towards life. As a stubborn only child, so I grew up doing nearly everything my own way. I also feel a real sense of achievement when I complete a project on my own. Plus, I have some money along the way!

Through my blog, I document projects I have completed, so others can learn from my attempts and do it themselves. I also have a member's area, where registered users can submit, vote and pay for projects they'd like to see complete.

A little more about me, you ask? Well I have just hit my 30s. I am currently self-employed, and split my time between data analysis and running my online business. I reside in the North West of the UK with my partner and my long-hair Chihuahua. We are expecting an addition to the family in the new year, as I am currently pregnant with our first child - I definitely draw the line at attempting the birth myself!
